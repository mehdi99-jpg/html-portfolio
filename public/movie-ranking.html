Best horror movies according to IMDB

The top 3 movies off all-time.

The Exorcist:

It is a classic horror film released in 1973, directed by William Friedkin and based on the 1971 novel of the same name by William Peter Blatty. The story revolves around the demonic possession of a young girl named Regan MacNeil, played by Linda Blair, and the two priests who attempt to exorcise the demon from her. The film is renowned for its intense and disturbing imagery, groundbreaking special effects, and psychological depth.

Hereditary:

It is a psychological horror film released in 2018, written and directed by Ari Aster in his feature directorial debut. The movie is noted for its unsettling atmosphere, complex narrative, and powerful performances, particularly by Toni Collette.

The Mist:

It is a 2007 science fiction horror film directed by Frank Darabont, based on Stephen King's 1980 novella of the same name. The film is renowned for its tense atmosphere, thought-provoking themes, and its controversial and shocking ending.
